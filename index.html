RPI HAT FLASH

A flash ring for your Raspberry Pi

Rev 00

The board is designed around the Ti TPS61169 WLED driver. The drivers are connected to the hardware PWM signals of the RPi. Keep in mind that you can't keep the LEDs on for a long time due to almost non-existant heat dissipation capacity of the PCB. The camera can be mounted in the center of the board. If you're using the default 11mm spacers you can fit the camera in the center of the board when folding the cable thightly.
